# Privacy Policy for CashPilot

Last updated: December 1, 2025

This privacy policy describes how CashPilot ("the App") handles your information.

## PERMISSIONS USED

Internet / Network state

- Used to load ads and fetch Remote Config values
- Your financial data is not sent to the developer

## ADVERTISING

We display ads using Google AdMob. Google may collect:

- Device identifiers
- Usage data for ad personalization

You can opt out of personalized ads in your device's Google settings.

## ANALYTICS

We do not use analytics or crash reporting SDKs.

## DATA STORAGE

- Transactions you add (amount, category, note, timestamp) are stored locally on your device
- App settings (currency, theme) are stored locally on your device
- We do not have servers storing your data
- We cannot see or access your files

## CHILDREN

This app is not intended for children under 13.

We may update this policy occasionally. Updates will be posted here.
